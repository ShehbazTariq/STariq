## Research Highlights

**Hybrid Quantum Computing for 6G and beyond**

* Prototype of a Big AI-Native Quantum Anonymous SC Framework for secure and private semantic communication.
* Deep Quantum-Transformer Networks for multimodal beam prediction in mmWave ISAC systems.

**Experimental Quantum Technologies**

* Quantum Approximate Optimization Algorithms on IBM Quantum Computers for task offloading in 6G URLLC scenarios.
* Quantum State Tomography with a measurement-setting count that scales linearly with Hilbert space dimension.

**Applications of Quantum Computing Algorithms**

* Multi-agent quantum reinforcement learning for digital twin placement in 6G multi-tier systems.
* QAOA for wireless and satellite communication problems, including constellation optimization and SWIPT in MIMO systems.
* Quantum error mitigation techniques for Pauli noise and quantum state tomography.
